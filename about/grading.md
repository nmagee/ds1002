# Assessments & Grading

Grading is by the usual ten-point 90-93 (A-), 94-97 (A), 98-100 (A+), etc. scale.

| Assessment | Weight |
|:-----------|:-------|
| Weekly homework   | 40%    |
| Weekly quizzes    | 60%    |

There will be no mid-term or final exams in this course.
