# Assessments & Grading

Grading is by a typical ten-point 90-93 (A-), 94-97 (A), 98-100 (A+), etc. scale.

| Assessment | Weight |
|:-----------|:-------|
| Weekly homework   | 50%    |
| Labs       | 10%    |
| Weekly quizzes    | 40%    |
